About HomeGadgetPro

💡 We help UK shoppers make smarter decisions about home appliances — without the jargon, without the bias, and without wasting money on the wrong product.

Who we are

HomeGadgetPro.co.uk is an independent UK product comparison site dedicated to home appliances. From coffee machines and air fryers to robot hoovers and speakers — we research, compare and score products so you don't have to.

We're not a retailer. We don't sell products. Our only goal is to help you find the right one.

What we do

We pull real product data from Amazon UK — including prices, ratings and customer reviews — and combine it with our own scoring system to give every product a HomeGadgetPro score out of 100. Our scores cover performance, value for money, ease of use, build quality and features.

We update our product listings weekly to make sure prices and ratings stay accurate.

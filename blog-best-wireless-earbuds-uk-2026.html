🎧 Wireless earbuds buying guide

What separates good earbuds from great ones:

  • ANC quality: Budget ANC (under £50) reduces constant hum like road noise or air conditioning. Premium ANC (Sony, Apple) also handles irregular noises like voices and traffic. If you commute by train, the difference is transformative.
  • Battery life: The quoted hours are per charge, not total. Add the case top-ups: a "7H + 20H case" = 27H total. Look for total playtime rather than per-charge figures.
  • Fit and seal: A proper in-ear seal is the biggest factor in both sound quality and passive noise isolation. Try different tip sizes — most earbuds include small, medium, and large.
  • Ecosystem compatibility: AirPods Pro work brilliantly with iPhone but are limited on Android. Samsung Galaxy Buds work best with Samsung phones. Soundcore, EarFun, and JBL work equally well on any phone.
  • Codec support: For Android users, aptX or LDAC gives noticeably better audio quality over standard AAC/SBC. AirPods use Apple's AAC which is optimised for iOS only.

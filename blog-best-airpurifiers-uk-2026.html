💨 How to choose the right air purifier

  • Room size: Match the stated coverage to your room. Undersizing means it can't keep up with air turnover — you'll barely notice the difference.
  • CADR rating: The higher the CADR (m³/h), the faster it cleans the air. For a 20m² room, aim for at least CADR 150.
  • True HEPA vs HEPA-style: True HEPA captures 99.97% of particles 0.3 microns+. This is what you want for allergens. "HEPA-style" is not the same thing.
  • Activated carbon layer: Removes odours and VOCs. Essential if you have pets, cook with strong spices, or live with a smoker.
  • Noise level: Most have a sleep/night mode around 25–30dB. Check reviews if noise is a concern — manufacturers often understate it.
  • Running costs: Replacement filters cost £15–£30 every 6–12 months. Add this to your total cost comparison.

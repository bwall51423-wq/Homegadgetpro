🧹 What to look for in a cordless vacuum

  • Suction power (KPa): 20–30KPa suits hard floors and light carpets. 40–50KPa handles deep pile carpets and pet hair properly.
  • Battery life: 20–30 minutes covers a flat. 45–65 minutes suits a full house. Check whether the stated time is at max suction or eco mode.
  • Weight: 2.5–3kg is typical. Under 2kg is genuinely lightweight. Matters a lot if you have stairs or a bad back.
  • HEPA filtration: Captures fine allergens. Important for allergy or asthma sufferers and pet owners.
  • Dustbin size: Small bins (0.5L) need frequent emptying. Aim for 0.8L+ if you have pets or large carpeted areas.

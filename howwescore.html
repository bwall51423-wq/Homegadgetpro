Methodology

How We Score

Every product on HomeGadgetPro gets a score out of 100. Here's exactly how we calculate it.

Our scoring system

The HomeGadgetPro score is a weighted combination of five factors. We pull real data from Amazon UK and use this to generate objective scores for each category.

⚡ Performance25%
How well the product does its core job, based on customer feedback and verified ratings.
💰 Value for Money25%
Price relative to performance and comparable products in the same category.
🎯 Ease of Use20%
How straightforward the product is to set up and use day to day.
🔧 Build Quality15%
Durability, materials and long term reliability indicated by customer reviews.
✨ Features15%
Range and quality of features relative to price point and category.

Our data sources

All product data including prices, ratings and customer review counts are sourced directly from Amazon UK and updated weekly. Verdicts and scoring breakdowns are generated using AI analysis of the available product data and customer feedback.

We always recommend reading customer reviews on Amazon before making a purchase, as individual experiences can vary.

Independence

Our scores are not influenced by brands, retailers or commission rates. A product with a lower affiliate commission rate receives the same fair scoring as one with a higher rate. See our Affiliate Disclosure for full details of our commercial relationships.

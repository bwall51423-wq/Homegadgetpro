🥤 Hand blender vs personal blender vs jug blender

The most important decision isn't the brand — it's the type:

  • Hand/stick blender: Best for soups, sauces and occasional smoothies. Cheapest. Blend directly in pans. Limited with frozen ingredients.
  • Personal blender (NutriBullet, Ninja cups): Best for daily smoothies. Compact, powerful, quick to clean. Single-serve only.
  • Jug blender: Best for batch cooking, hot soups, family-sized smoothies. Handles large volumes and heat. Takes more space.

For most people making morning smoothies, a personal blender is the right choice. If you cook a lot and want one tool that does everything, a hand blender is more practical day-to-day.

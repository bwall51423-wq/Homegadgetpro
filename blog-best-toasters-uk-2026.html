🍞 Toaster buying guide

A few features genuinely make a difference to daily use:

  • Slot width: Standard slots (24–25mm) handle most sliced bread. Extra-wide slots (35–40mm) accommodate thick artisan loaves, crumpets and bagels. Worth having.
  • High lift: Extends the carriage upward so you can safely grab small items without burning fingers. Essential for crumpets, small slices, and English muffins.
  • Browning settings: 5–7 settings provides good control. The difference of one setting can mean the difference between golden and burnt.
  • Defrost mode: Defrosts frozen bread or crumpets without burning the outside. More useful than it sounds if you keep bread in the freezer.
  • Cancel button: Stops toasting mid-cycle. Sounds obvious — not all budget models have it.
  • 2-slice vs 4-slice: 4-slice only makes sense if you regularly toast for 3+ people simultaneously. It takes up significantly more counter space.
